Classic Waffle

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Granulated sugar
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Unsalted butter, melted
  • Vanilla extract

Method of preparation

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, granulated sugar, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Then add in the milk, melted unsalted butter, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are fine.
  4. Preheat your waffle iron according to its instructions. Once heated, lightly grease the waffle iron with cooking spray or melted butter.
  5. Spoon the waffle batter onto the preheated waffle iron, using the recommended amount for your specific waffle iron size.
  6. Cook the waffles until golden brown and crisp, following the instructions provided with your waffle iron.
  7. Carefully remove the waffles from the iron and serve them warm.
  8. You can top your classic waffles with maple syrup, fresh fruits, whipped cream, or any other desired toppings.

Enjoy your classic waffle!
